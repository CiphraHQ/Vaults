The Privacy Problem with AI

Current AI tools require sending your data to the cloud. For professionals handling sensitive information, that's not an option.

Cloud AI Vulnerability

Cloud AI tools like ChatGPT require uploading your data to external servers. This violates client confidentiality, professional ethics, and regulatory requirements.

Local Processing

Vaults runs entirely on your device. Your data never touches the internet—it's processed locally with the same AI capabilities, but with complete privacy.

Security Architecture In Development

Detailed security documentation will be available before the beta release. Here's what we can share about our approach:

Core Security Principles

  • Zero Trust Architecture: No data ever leaves your device
  • End-to-End Encryption: AES-256 encryption for all stored data
  • Local Processing: All AI computation happens on your hardware
  • No Telemetry: No usage tracking or analytics

Technical Implementation

Full technical specifications will be published before beta release. We're working with security professionals to ensure enterprise-grade protection.

System Requirements Preliminary

These are preliminary requirements based on current development. Final specifications will be available closer to beta release.

Minimum Requirements (Estimated)

  • RAM: 8GB (16GB recommended)
  • Storage: 10GB free space
  • CPU: Modern multi-core processor (2018 or newer)
  • OS: Windows 10+, macOS 12+, or Linux (Ubuntu 20.04+)

Recommended for Best Performance

  • RAM: 32GB or more
  • GPU: Dedicated graphics card (for faster AI processing)
  • Storage: SSD with 50GB+ free space

Note: Requirements may change as we optimize the local AI models. We're working to make Vaults accessible on a wide range of hardware.
