The Privacy Problem with AI

Current AI tools require sending your data to the cloud. For professionals handling sensitive information, that's not an option.

Cloud AI Vulnerability

Cloud AI tools like ChatGPT require uploading your data to external servers. This violates client confidentiality, professional ethics, and regulatory requirements.

Local Processing

Vaults runs entirely on your device. Your data never touches the internet—it's processed locally with the same AI capabilities, but with complete privacy.

What Vaults Does

A complete AI workspace designed for professionals who need absolute privacy.

AI

Local AI & Transcription

Advanced language models and speech-to-text running entirely on your device. Chat with AI, transcribe interviews, and process audio—all without internet connectivity.

Document Analysis

AI-powered summarization and analysis of your sensitive documents. Extract insights, generate reports, and understand complex materials while maintaining complete confidentiality.
